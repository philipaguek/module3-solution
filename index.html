Our Menu

Chicken Dishes

Our chicken dishes are prepared using only the finest, tender cuts of chicken. Each dish is carefully seasoned with a variety of herbs and spices to bring out the rich flavors and create a truly mouthwatering experience. Whether you prefer it grilled, fried, or roasted, we have something for every chicken lover!

  • Grilled Chicken: Tender and juicy chicken breast, grilled to perfection with a smoky finish.
  • Fried Chicken: Crispy on the outside, juicy on the inside, our fried chicken is a crowd favorite.
  • Roast Chicken: A flavorful whole chicken slow-roasted to golden perfection with a blend of herbs and spices.

Beef Dishes

Our beef dishes are crafted with the finest cuts of meat to provide a savory, satisfying experience. From juicy steaks to hearty beef stews, our beef menu is designed for those who crave a flavorful, filling meal. Each dish is prepared with care and attention to detail.

  • Grilled Steak: Perfectly seared steak, served with your choice of side dish and sauces.
  • Beef Stew: A rich and hearty stew made with tender chunks of beef, root vegetables, and a savory broth.
  • Beef Burger: A deliciously juicy beef patty grilled to perfection and served with all the classic toppings.

Sushi

Our sushi menu features the freshest seafood, hand-picked from local suppliers to ensure the highest quality. From classic nigiri to creative rolls, our sushi selection will take your taste buds on a journey through Japan's finest flavors.

  • California Roll: A mix of crab meat, avocado, and cucumber, rolled in seaweed and rice.
  • Sashimi: Fresh slices of raw fish served with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
  • Tempura Roll: A crispy roll filled with shrimp tempura, avocado, and a spicy mayo sauce.
